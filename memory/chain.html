Let's start memorizing!
Reception of memorization by the Chain. On the screen words will be displayed in order.
Words are remembered as follows: the first word is mentally connected to the second, the second to the third, the third to the fourth, and so on. It turns out that words are connected in the same way as links in a chain, hence the name of the method.
You need to pair the words together in pairs: in the imagination there must be only two images at once, the entire chain should not be presented at once. In order that when recalling it would be easy to recall the order of words, put the image of the second word in the imagination from above the first, on the right side of the first, or the second image should penetrate into the first.
The images in pairs follow to represent approximately the same size. Images must necessarily contact.
